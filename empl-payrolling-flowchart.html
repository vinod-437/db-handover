Empl-Payrolling Flowchart

A visual breakdown of the page logic

        graph TD
            Start[Initialization] --> Extract[Extract URL Params]
            Extract --> FetchMaster[Fetch Master Data]
            FetchMaster --> ActionBranch{Action Param?}
            
            ActionBranch -->|salary| Salary[Load customSalaryform]
            ActionBranch -->|EPF or ESI| Compliance[Load statutoryComplianceForm]
            ActionBranch -->|exit| ExitModal[Open Exit Form]
            ActionBranch -->|experience| ExpModal[Open Experience Form]
            ActionBranch -->|consultant| ConModal[Open Consultant Form]

            Salary --> CalcSal[Calculate Math Split]
            CalcSal --> GetGrossAPI[API call: getuspccalcgrossfromctc]
            GetGrossAPI --> DisplaySal[Display Result In Table]
            DisplaySal --> SaveSal[User clicks Save]
            SaveSal --> SaveSalAPI[API call: saveCustomSalaryStructure]

            Compliance --> SaveStat[User Clicks Save]
            SaveStat --> SaveStatAPI[API call: save statutory compliance]

            ExitModal --> SaveExit[API call: saveExitEmployee]
            
            ExpModal --> GenExp[Generate Letter]
            ConModal --> SaveCon[Save Consultant API]
